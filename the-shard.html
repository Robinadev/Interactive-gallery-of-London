About The Shard

The Shard, also referred to as the Shard of Glass, is a 95-story supertall skyscraper in Southwark, London. Standing 309.6 meters (1,016 feet) high, it is the tallest building in the United Kingdom, and the seventh-tallest building in Europe.

Designed by architect Renzo Piano, The Shard's tapered form emerges from the Thames and was inspired by the spires of London churches and the masts of sailing ships that once docked in the Pool of London. The building offers mixed-use space including offices, restaurants, the Shangri-La Hotel, and The View from The Shard observation deck.
